## Writing Approach

Troubleshooting and support documentation follows these principles:
- **Diagnostic Framework:** Helping users identify their specific issue
- **Step-by-Step Guidance:** Clear procedures for resolution or setup
- **Prerequisites:** Clear communication of what's needed
- **Multiple Approaches:** Different solutions for different scenarios
- **Verification Methods:** How to confirm successful completion
- **User Empathy:** Acknowledging frustration and complexity
- **Technical Depth:** Sufficient detail for accurate problem-solving
- **Cross-Reference:** Links to related documentation
