## Writing Approach

All best practices guides follow these principles:
- **Scenario-Based:** Real-world situations, not abstract theory
- **Multiple Approaches:** Recognizing different valid solutions
- **Trade-Off Analysis:** Clear communication of pros and cons
- **Decision Support:** Helping users choose the right approach
- **Practical Implementation:** Not just theory, but how to execute
- **Context Awareness:** Understanding organizational needs and constraints
