## Writing Approach

All instructional guides in this portfolio follow these principles:
- **Scannable Structure:** Users can quickly find what they need
- **Prerequisite Focus:** Clear communication of requirements upfront
- **Step Clarity:** Each step is discrete and actionable
- **Verification:** Methods to confirm successful completion
- **Accessibility:** Language suitable for target audience
